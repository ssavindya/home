Sinhala & Tamil New Year

May this festive season bring joy, prosperity, and bright new beginnings.

සිංහල අලුත් අවුරුද්දට සුබ පැතුම් May your year be blessed with peace, fortune, and new beginnings.
தமிழ்புத்தாண்டு வாழ்த்துக்கள் May the harvest of joy and success grow abundantly in your life.

Celebrate with family, share traditional treats, and welcome the new year with hope.
